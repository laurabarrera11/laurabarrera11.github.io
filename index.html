HotPlate

HotPlate is an app that allows you to answer the question of what to order at a restaurant. I joined this startup during my 2017 Spring quarter as a software developer. Alongside another developer we built an iOS and Android mobile app using Facebook’s React Native framework and JavaScript. I worked on all the backend functionality of the app, such as account creation and management, rating calculations and storage in the database (Firebase). I also worked on the search algorithm taking factors such as rakings, ratings, tags, and distance for each dish into account.

Potato Ventures, Inc.

What would happen if Facebook, LinkedIn, Slack and AngelList had a child that went to college? Potato is a React web platform that allows college students to make the most out of their 4 years in college by connecting them to startups and student groups on campus, as well as tracking and managing the application process. I am currently UI/UX lead for the team and have been working on all the frontend of the website. Additionally, I have been leading the development in React Native of a complementary mobile app that allows users to communicate with others through a campus wide messaging platform.
